Privacy Policy for pix2d.com

At pix2d.com (the “Site”), we respect your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect when you visit the Site and how we use it. This policy covers only the Site; for the Pix2D application, please see the app privacy policy.

Information we collect

  • Server logs. Like most websites, our hosting provider records standard log data when you access the Site: IP address, user agent, date/time, requested URL, and referrer. We use this to maintain security, prevent abuse, and understand site performance.
  • Cookies and similar technologies. We use cookies to operate the Site and to measure usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.
  • Transactional data. If you purchase a Pix2D license via our payment provider (Tinkoff), the payment is processed by Tinkoff on their servers. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. We may receive limited information such as your order ID, payment status, and contact email (if provided) for the purpose of fulfilling your order and providing support.

Analytics and third-party services

We use the following third-party services to help operate the Site and understand usage:

  • Google Analytics 4 (via gtag.js) – helps us measure and analyze traffic and usage patterns. Learn more at Google’s Privacy & Terms and Analytics Opt-out.
  • Yandex.Metrica – may be used for additional analytics and performance insights. Learn more at Yandex Privacy Policy and opt-out information.
  • Tinkoff Payments – our payment provider for license purchases. Learn more at Tinkoff Privacy Policy.

These providers may set their own cookies or collect information as described in their policies above. We do not sell your personal information.

How we use information

  • Operate, secure, and improve the Site.
  • Analyze aggregated usage to make better product and content decisions.
  • Process and support license purchases and related customer service.

Your choices

  • You can manage cookies via your browser settings and the opt-out links above for analytics.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data you believe we hold about you.

Data retention

We retain server logs and analytics data for as long as needed for the purposes above and to comply with legal obligations. Transactional records are kept as required for accounting and support.

Children’s privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the most recent changes.
